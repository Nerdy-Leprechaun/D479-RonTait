Lodging in Taniti

From budget-friendly hostels to family-owned hotels and a four-star resort, Taniti offers lodging options for every kind of traveler. All lodging is regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Lodging Options

Choose the stay that fits your style and budget. Many visitors stay near Taniti City or Merriton Landing for easy access to beaches, dining, and nightlife.

Hostel

An inexpensive hostel ideal for solo travelers and small groups looking to keep costs low and meet other visitors.

  • Dorm-style rooms
  • Shared common areas
  • Walkable to buses and beaches

Family-Owned Hotels

Small, family-owned hotels offering local hospitality and convenient access to Taniti City and Yellow Leaf Bay.

  • Cozy rooms with basic amenities
  • Often near restaurants and shops
  • Great for couples and small families
