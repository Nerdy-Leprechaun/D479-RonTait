Before You Visit

  • Power outlets are 120 volts (US standard).
  • Alcohol is not sold or served between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
  • The drinking age is 18 and lightly enforced.
  • Currency: US dollars, with many places accepting euros and yen.
  • English is widely spoken in Taniti City.
  • Many attractions and restaurants close on national holidays.
