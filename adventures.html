Adventures & Activities

From relaxing days on the beach to active hikes, volcano tours, snorkeling, and zip-lining, Taniti offers adventures for a wide range of visitors.

Things to Do in Taniti

Mix and match activities to create your ideal trip, or focus on one area—outdoor adventure, culture, or nightlife.

Beaches

Several beaches welcome visitors, including sandy stretches near Yellow Leaf Bay and other coastal areas.

Volcano Tours

Guided trips to the island’s active volcano, with scenic views and educational insights into Taniti’s geography.

Rainforest Hikes

Hike through lush rainforest environments, with opportunities for zip-lining and wildlife viewing.

Snorkeling

Snorkeling opportunities around Yellow Leaf Bay and nearby reefs let you explore underwater life.

Chartered Fishing

Go out with local guides on chartered fishing tours to experience Taniti’s surrounding waters.

Merriton Landing Nightlife

A small but growing boardwalk with nightlife options and entertainment along the waterfront.

Museums & Culture

Visit the history museum, small local museums, and sites with native architecture in Taniti City.
