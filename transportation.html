Getting Around Taniti

Buses, taxis, rental cars, and bikes make it easy to move between Taniti City, Yellow Leaf Bay, the rainforest, and other areas of the island.

Arriving in Taniti

Airport

A small airport accommodates small jets, with expansion planned. Most visitors connect through regional hubs before arriving in Taniti.

Cruise Ship

A cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay once a week, staying for one night. Visitors can explore the island during shore excursions.

On-Island Transportation

Public Buses

Public buses operate in Taniti City from 5:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m., offering an affordable way to travel around the city center.

Private Buses

Private buses serve the rest of the island, connecting beaches, sightseeing spots, and outlying areas.

Taxis

Taxis are available throughout Taniti City, offering flexible transportation for individuals and small groups.

Rental Cars

Rental cars are available near the airport for travelers who want the freedom to explore the island on their own schedule.

Bikes

Bicycles can be rented in several locations. Helmets are required by law and provided by bike rental companies.

Walking

Taniti City and Merriton Landing are highly walkable, with many attractions, restaurants, and lodgings within a short distance.
