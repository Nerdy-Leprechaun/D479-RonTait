Sightseeing in Taniti

Explore Taniti City, Yellow Leaf Bay, native architecture, and learning opportunities at local museums.

Taniti City & Culture

Native Architecture

Stroll through Taniti City to see buildings that reflect traditional and modern influences across the island’s history.

Museums

Visit the history museum and several small museums to learn about Taniti’s culture, environment, and past.

City Walking Tours

Take guided or self-guided walking tours through the city streets, markets, and public spaces.

Yellow Leaf Bay & Coastline

Enjoy scenic views of the coastline and bay areas, with opportunities for photography, relaxation, and beach walks.

Bay Overlooks

Scenic overlooks near Yellow Leaf Bay provide views of the water, cruise ships, and surrounding landscape.

Boardwalk & Waterfront

Merriton Landing’s small but growing boardwalk area includes shops, dining, and nightlife along the water.

Beach Walks

Several beaches offer peaceful walks along the shoreline, with easy access from many lodging areas.
